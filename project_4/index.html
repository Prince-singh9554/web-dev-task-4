Top Attractions

Prem Mandir

Prem Mandir is a grand temple in Vrindavan, dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual ambiance.

Ram Mandir

Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Ram, symbolizing faith, devotion, and cultural heritage.

Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is a spiritual hub on the banks of the Ganges, known for its temples, ghats, and rich cultural heritage.

Golden temple

The Golden Temple in Amritsar is a revered Sikh shrine, renowned for its stunning architecture, spiritual significance, and peaceful ambiance.

India Gate

India Gate in New Delhi is a majestic war memorial, honoring soldiers who died during World War I and reflecting India's national pride and heritage.

Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi is a historic Mughal fort, known for its stunning red sandstone architecture and significant role in India's history..
